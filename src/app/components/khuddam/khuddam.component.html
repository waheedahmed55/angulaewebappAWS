Message From Qaid Malis

Dear Brothers, As the year of 2019 comes to an end we must reflect on our past year, and our future steps. When individuals set goals for the new year there is great planning but remember that none of this is possible without the grace of Allah. Hazrat Khalifatul Masih V (atba) has constantly reminded us to reflect on our relationship with Allah, and our pledge of Bai'at to the Promised Messiah (as). How much we fulfilled our pledges last year and if there were any deficiencies how can we make good this year? As Khuddam we recite the pledge "I shall sacrifice my life, wealth, time and honour for the sake of my faith, country and nation" 2020 requires you to come forward ready to fulfill your religious obligations even more-so than last year. May Allah grant us peace and success going forward, Ameen! Wasalaam, Waheed Ahmed

Shahjehan Ahmad.
